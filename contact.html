Our Team

Welcome to the team! Our team consists of a passionate group of individuals dedicated to innovation, technology, and social responsibility. Together, we connected our diverse abilities to create the Second Chance to aid drunk driving issues prevailing around our society. Explore below to meet our team members and learn more about their roles in our innovative project.

Our motivation is deeply rooted in personal experiences and a collective commitment to combat drunk driving. Luke's traumatic loss of his dog, Coco, to a drunk driving incident at a young age serves as a poignant reminder of the devastating consequences. This heartbreaking event has fueled our determination to innovate 'The Second Chance,' an impactful solution. Beyond individual stories, our team's diverse skills coalesce to address this societal issue, combining technology, business strategy, and artistic flair. 'The Second Chance' is not just a project; it's a passionate response to save lives and foster responsible driving habits.
